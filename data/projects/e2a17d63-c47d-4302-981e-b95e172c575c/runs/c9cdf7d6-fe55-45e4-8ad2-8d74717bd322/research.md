## Key Findings Summary
For a secure todo app with login and projects, use HTTPS, validate inputs, and manage user authentication securely. Follow secure coding best practices and regularly update dependencies.

Risks include data security, scalability issues, and potential dependency on third-party services. Constraints involve limited customization and user experience challenges. Edge cases may involve task prioritization and sorting of user-created tasks.
